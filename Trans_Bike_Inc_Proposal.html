Proposal: Addressing Employee Concerns at Trans-Bike Inc.

Dear Management Team,

As the Head of the HR Department at Trans-Bike Inc., I would like to propose concrete strategies to address the top three concerns identified by our departing employees during 2019–2020: Salary Issues, Lack of Promotion Opportunities, and High Work Pressure.

1. Salary Issues:

  • A. Conduct a comprehensive salary analysis to identify any gaps between our current salary packages and market standards.
  • B. Develop a structured salary increase plan based on performance, experience, and market research findings.
  • C. Regularly review and adjust salaries to ensure they remain competitive and aligned with industry benchmarks.

2. Lack of Promotion Opportunities:

  • A. Implement a career development program that offers employees clear growth paths and opportunities for advancement.
  • B. Establish mentorship programs to provide guidance and support for employees seeking career progression.
  • C. Encourage cross–functional training and job rotations to enhance employees' skills and prepare them for future leadership roles.

3. High Work Pressure:

  • A. Conduct a workload assessment to Identify areas of excessive pressure and workload imbalance.
  • B. Explore the possibility of hiring additional staff or redistributing tasks to alleviate work pressure.
  • C. Implement stress management programs, such as mindfulness workshops and wellness initiatives, to promote a healthy work–life balance.

By implementing these strategies, we aim to address the key concerns raised by our departing employees and foster a more positive and engaging work environment at Trans–Bike Inc. These initiatives will help us attract and retain top talent while fostering employee satisfaction, growth, and productivity.

Thank you for considering these strategies. We are confident that they will contribute to the long–term success of our company.

Sincerely,


Kelly

Head of HR Department

Trans–Bike Inc.
