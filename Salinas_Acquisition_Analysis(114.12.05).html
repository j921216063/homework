On Monday, Salinas Products, a large food distributor based in Mexico City, announced its plans to acquire the Pablo’s restaurant chain. Pablo Benavidez, the chain’s owner, had been considering holding an auction for ownership of the chain. He ultimately made the decision to sell to Salinas without seeking other offers. He opened a small food stand in his hometown of Cancún. Following that, he opened restaurants in Puerto Vallarta and Veracruz, and there are now over 50 Pablo’s restaurants nationwide.

文法亮點：同位語與分詞構句

這句有兩個補充說明結構：(1) 同位語 "a large food distributor..." 用來指稱 Salinas Products 的身分；(2) 過去分詞片語 "based in Mexico City" 用來修飾 distributor，省略了關代與 Be 動詞 (which is based in...)。

文法亮點：不定詞當形容詞

"to sell to Salinas" 是不定詞片語當形容詞用，修飾前面的名詞 "decision"（決定要做什麼）。另外 "without" 是介系詞，後面必須接動名詞 (V-ing) "seeking"。

文法亮點：同意做某事 (agree to V)

動詞 "agree" 後面如果要接動作，習慣使用不定詞 "to V" (to keep)。

文法亮點：時間副詞的堆疊

這句話有兩個時間副詞片語："40 years ago" 與 "right after..."。在英文中，時間通常放在句尾。"Right" 在這裡是副詞，用來強調 "after" (就在...之後)。

文法亮點：介系詞 of 表示「...的」或「即為」

"hometown of Cancún" 中的 of 有點類似同位語的功能，表示「坎昆這個家鄉」(the hometown which is Cancún)。

Participle Phr (Adv) Following that, S he V opened O restaurants ... and S (虛主詞) there V are S (真主詞) now over 50 Pablo’s restaurants Adv nationwide.

隨後，他在巴亞爾塔港和韋拉克魯斯開設了餐廳，現在全國有超過 50 家 Pablo's 餐廳。

文法亮點：現在分詞作轉折詞 & 虛主詞

(1) "Following that" 雖然結構上是分詞片語（隨後、接著），但在新聞英文中常當作時間轉折詞使用。(2) "There are..." 是虛主詞句型，動詞 "are" 由後面的複數真主詞 "restaurants" 決定。
