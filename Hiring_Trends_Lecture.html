1. 原文閱讀 (Original Text)

In today's competitive job market, the traditional focus on monetary compensation alone does not fully encapsulate what potential employees seek. More and more, individuals are gravitating towards opportunities that cater to the comprehensive needs of their families, align with their personal values, and enhance their future skill sets. It's no longer just about securing employment, let alone a sizable paycheck. As a Hiring Manager, I must adopt a broader perspective to understand these nuanced needs, engaging in open dialogues about work–life balance, ethical company practices, and professional growth opportunities. This holistic approach to talent acquisition will not only enhance the quality of our hires but will also reinforce our reputation as an employer who truly understands and values their workforce.
